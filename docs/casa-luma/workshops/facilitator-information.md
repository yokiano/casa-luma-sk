# Workshop Facilitator Information

This document is the current source of truth for practical information we share with people who are interested in hosting workshops at Casa Luma.

Status: early draft. Some details may change as we learn more from facilitators and refine the workshop flow.

## Overview

Casa Luma is beginning to offer its spaces for external facilitators to host workshops, classes, and small group experiences. We are currently learning what facilitators need, what formats work best in our space, and how to make the process clear for everyone.

Public workshop inquiries should go to `info@casalumakpg.com`.

## Available spaces

### Ground-floor workshop room

The primary workshop space is the new ground-floor workshop room.

- Location: ground floor.
- Size: approximately the size of a bedroom.
- Suggested capacity: up to 8 participants for yoga or movement-based sessions that need floor space.
- Current equipment: cushions are available.
- Yoga equipment: we do not currently provide yoga mats or dedicated yoga props.
- Space visits: facilitators who want to see the room before deciding can ask us, and we will arrange for someone to show them the space.

This is currently the preferred space for workshops.

### Roof / attic-level space

There is also an upper roof/attic-level area with much more open space.

However, from our experience, workshops there are usually only practical in the early morning or toward the end of the day. The wooden floor carries footstep noise to the play area below, which can disturb families and children using the play space.

For now, we prefer to keep most workshops in the new workshop room. If a facilitator needs more space or a larger participant capacity, we can discuss whether the upper space is suitable for the specific workshop format and time.

## Music and sound

Casa Luma can arrange a music playback setup if needed. Facilitators should tell us in advance if they need music or audio support.

## Promotion

Approved workshops can be added to Casa Luma's schedule. Casa Luma has a steady flow of families looking for activities, community events, and knowledge-sharing opportunities. This should be communicated as practical visibility, not as a promise of sales or attendance.

## Pricing and time blocks

The current model is a fixed workshop space rental:

- Price: 1,500 THB per workshop block.
- Included time: up to 2 hours.

The 2-hour block is intended to cover the full use of the space, not just the active workshop time. For example:

- Room preparation.
- Participant arrival and gathering.
- The workshop itself, which is often around 1 hour.
- Packing up and resetting the room before the next activity.

We are open to hearing how this model fits facilitators' expectations and needs.

## Legal and operational responsibility

Casa Luma's current model is that we rent the space to the workshop operator/facilitator.

The facilitator is responsible for:

- Any required work permits or legal permissions.
- The workshop activity itself.
- Participant management and safety.
- Any professional requirements, insurance, certifications, or approvals relevant to their activity.

Casa Luma provides the space according to the agreed time block and setup.
